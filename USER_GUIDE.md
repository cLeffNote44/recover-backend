## Getting Started

### First Launch

When you first open the app, you'll be prompted to set your sobriety date. This is the foundation of your recovery tracking.

**To set your sobriety date:**
1. Click "Change Date" on the home screen
2. Select your sobriety start date
3. Click "Save"

Your days sober counter will now display the correct number of days.

### üìù Managing Cravings

**When a Craving Hits:**

1. **Immediate Action:**
   - Take 3 deep breaths
   - Remember: Cravings pass (usually 15-30 minutes)
   - Use the Emergency button if needed

2. **Log the Craving:**
   - Go to **Journal** tab
   - Select **Cravings** sub-tab
   - Click "Log Craving"
   - Fill out the form:
     - **Intensity:** Slide to rate 1-10
     - **Trigger:** Select what caused it
     - **Notes:** Describe the situation
     - **Coping Strategy:** What you did to manage it
     - **Success:** Check if you overcame it
   - Click "Save"

3. **Use Coping Strategies:**
   - Click the **Emergency** button (top-right)
   - Review the 10 coping strategies
   - Choose one and implement it
   - Call your sponsor if needed

**Why Log Cravings:**
- Identify your trigger patterns
- Track what coping strategies work
- See your success rate improve
- Gain insights in Analytics

### üõ°Ô∏è Relapse Prevention Plan

**Building Your Plan:**

1. Click "Prevention Plan" on Home tab
2. Full-screen editor opens
3. Complete each section:

**Section 1: Warning Signs**
- What behaviors indicate you're at risk?
- Examples: Skipping meetings, isolating, irritability
- Add as many as relevant to you

**Section 2: High-Risk Situations**
- What situations trigger you?
- Examples: Bars, certain people, stress, loneliness
- Be specific and honest

**Section 3: Green Zone (Safe)**
- What keeps you stable?
- Examples: Daily meetings, sponsor calls, exercise
- Your maintenance routine

**Section 4: Yellow Zone (Caution)**
- What to do when warning signs appear?
- Examples: Extra meetings, therapy, avoid triggers
- Early intervention steps

**Section 5: Red Zone (Danger)**
- Emergency protocols
- Examples: Call sponsor NOW, go to meeting, ER
- Crisis response plan

4. Click "Save Plan"

**Using Your Plan:**
- Review monthly (set a calendar reminder)
- Update as you learn more about yourself
- Share with sponsor or therapist
- Access quickly when needed

## Troubleshooting

### Data Issues

**Data Not Saving:**
- Check browser localStorage is enabled
- Try a different browser
- Export data as backup
- Clear cache and try again

**Lost Data:**
- Import from most recent export
- Check if browser cleared data
- Ensure localStorage wasn't disabled

### App Issues

**App Not Loading:**
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection (first load)
- Try incognito/private mode

**Features Not Working:**
- Update to latest browser version
- Disable browser extensions
- Check console for errors
- Try different browser

### PWA Issues

**Can't Install:**
- Use supported browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge)
- Ensure HTTPS connection
- Check device storage
- Try desktop vs mobile

**Offline Not Working:**
- Visit app while online first
- Check service worker is registered
- Clear cache and reload
- Reinstall PWA
