### 3. Risk Prediction & Analytics

AI-powered predictive system that identifies relapse risk patterns.

#### Risk Assessment Algorithm
Analyzes multiple data points:
- **Check-in consistency** - Missed check-ins indicate disengagement
- **Mood trends** - Declining mood patterns raise alerts
- **Craving frequency** - Increased cravings signal vulnerability
- **Meeting attendance** - Reduced support system engagement
- **Time in recovery** - Early recovery has higher statistical risk

#### Risk Levels
- **Low Risk** (Green) - Strong patterns, no intervention shown
- **Medium Risk** (Yellow) - Warning signs detected, suggestions provided
- **High Risk** (Red) - Multiple risk factors, urgent recommendations

#### Proactive Interventions
When medium/high risk detected:
- **Personalized recommendations**
  - "Schedule a meeting with your sponsor"
  - "Review your relapse prevention plan"
  - "Practice a coping skill"
  - "Call a support contact"
- **Emergency support quick access**
- **Gentle nudges, not judgmental**

#### Analytics Dashboard
- **Mood Trends:** 7/30/90-day averages with charts
- **Check-in Consistency:** Streak calendar visualization
- **Craving Patterns:** Time-of-day and trigger analysis
- **Progress Metrics:** Comprehensive recovery statistics

**Location:** Home Screen (when risk detected), Analytics Tab

### 10. HALT Assessment

Quick self-assessment for the four common relapse triggers:

#### HALT States

**H - Hungry**
- Physical hunger impacts decision-making
- Blood sugar affects mood and impulse control
- **Recommendations:**
  - Eat a healthy snack
  - Plan regular meals
  - Keep healthy foods available

**A - Angry**
- Unprocessed anger is a relapse risk
- Resentment builds over time
- **Recommendations:**
  - Practice deep breathing
  - Physical exercise to release tension
  - Talk it out with someone
  - Use cognitive restructuring

**L - Lonely**
- Isolation is dangerous in recovery
- Humans need connection
- **Recommendations:**
  - Call a friend from program
  - Attend a meeting
  - Reach out to sponsor
  - Join recovery community

**T - Tired**
- Fatigue weakens resolve
- Sleep deprivation impairs judgment
- **Recommendations:**
  - Take a nap if possible
  - Go to bed early tonight
  - Practice sleep hygiene
  - Reduce commitments

#### Integration
- **Check-in component** - Included in daily check-ins
- **Quick assessment** - Standalone tool anytime
- **Risk prediction** - HALT states feed into risk algorithm
- **Historical tracking** - See patterns over time
- **Proactive alerts** - "You've been tired 3 days in a row..."

**Location:** Daily Check-In, Prevention Tab

## Planning & Prevention

### 24. Relapse Prevention Plan

Structured planning tool for identifying and managing relapse risks.

#### Plan Components

**1. Warning Signs**
Identify your early relapse indicators:
- **Behavioral** - Isolating, skipping meetings
- **Emotional** - Irritability, depression, anxiety
- **Cognitive** - Romanticizing use, minimizing consequences
- **Physical** - Sleep changes, appetite changes

**2. High-Risk Situations**
Know your triggers:
- **People** - Old using friends, toxic relationships
- **Places** - Bars, old neighborhoods, dealer locations
- **Things** - Substances visible, paraphernalia
- **Times** - Anniversaries, holidays, stressful periods
- **Emotions** - Anger, loneliness, celebration

**3. Green Zone Actions (Low Risk)**
Maintain wellness:
- Daily check-ins
- Regular meetings
- Healthy routines
- Self-care practices
- Support network engagement

**4. Yellow Zone Actions (Warning Signs)**
Intervene early:
- Call sponsor
- Attend extra meeting
- Review reasons for sobriety
- Practice coping skills
- Increase support

**5. Red Zone Actions (High Risk/Crisis)**
Emergency response:
- Call crisis hotline
- Go to safe place
- Contact emergency contact
- Attend meeting immediately
- Seek professional help

#### Plan Creation Wizard
- **Guided setup** - Step-by-step questions
- **Examples provided** - See common warning signs
- **Personalization** - Tailor to your situation
- **Review prompts** - Update quarterly

#### Plan Usage
- **Quick reference** - Always accessible
- **Emergency mode** - One-tap access when in crisis
- **Reminder notifications** - Review plan weekly
- **Share with support** - Give copy to sponsor/therapist

**Location:** Prevention Tab > Relapse Plan
