### **2. HALT Check-In System** âœ¨ NEW
Evidence-based relapse prevention tool identifying four common triggers.

**What is HALT?**
HALT is an acronym for Hungry, Angry, Lonely, Tired - four physical and emotional states that increase relapse risk in recovery.

**Features:**
- Four sliders (1-10 scale) for each HALT factor:
  - **Hungry:** Physical needs and nutrition
  - **Angry:** Emotional state and frustration levels
  - **Lonely:** Social connection and isolation
  - **Tired:** Energy levels and sleep quality
- Automatic risk level calculation:
  - Low Risk (score < 20): Green indicator
  - Medium Risk (score 20-27): Yellow indicator
  - High Risk (score â‰¥ 28): Red indicator
- Context-aware suggestions for high factors (â‰¥7):
  - Specific action items for each factor
  - Evidence-based coping strategies
  - Immediate steps to take
- Visual feedback with icons and colors
- Optional integration in daily check-ins
- Required for craving logging

**Integration Points:**
- Available during daily check-ins (optional)
- Required when logging cravings
- Data flows into HALT analytics dashboard

**Benefits:**
- Early warning system for relapse risk
- Identifies underlying triggers before cravings escalate

- Tracks patterns over time

**Traffic Light System Explained:**
